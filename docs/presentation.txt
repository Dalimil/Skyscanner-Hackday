Conferences, festivals and other events
Problems:
1) People come from different countries and arrive at different times
2) You have to ask everyone when they arrive - you don't have their flight info
3) Many people don't come at all because they find the organization too complicated

Our web application solves all of these problems:
1) You can easily set up groups and invite your friends
2) Optimal flight is found for each user - minimize both cost and time of arrival
3) You can see when your friends are coming
